About

CommunityPulse allows multi-level exploration of community input

As the community engagement increases, it becomes difficult to sift through and understand large number of community input to make decisions.

CommunityPulse surfaces community reactions, identifies key points of opinions, and allows exploration of multifaceted comments by combining text analysis and visual analytics wrapped in an interactive interface.
